About MaiGuide

MaiGuide is an innovative AI-powered life coach and goal-setting application designed to help you achieve your best self. By leveraging advanced AI technologies, MaiGuide listens to your conversations, observes your health data from devices like smartwatches, and provides timely feedback to enhance your communication and behavior.

Key Features

  • Real-time feedback to improve your speech and behavior
  • Personalized goal-setting and progress tracking
  • Integration with wearable devices to monitor health and habits
  • AI-driven insights to identify opportunities for personal growth
  • On-the-spot coaching to help you be your best self
